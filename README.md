Police Safe Complaint App
An AI-powered anonymous complaint platform that enables citizens to report crimes safely
without revealing their identity.
The app allows users to submit complaints via text or voice in local languages, while AI
automatically analyzes urgency and classifies the severity of the case. Complaints are securely
encrypted and routed directly to the nearest police authority.
